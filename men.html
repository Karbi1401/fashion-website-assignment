Top 10 Spring/Summer 2022 Men's Fashion Trends

Summer will be a breeze with K&K's handpicked trends from the Spring/Summer 2022 men's fashion shows.

Every rising trend on the Spring/Summer 2022 men's runways appears to have been put in place to get you out of the lengthy, never-ending winter. Airy silhouettes, dramatic cut-outs, and a casual aesthetic are just three of the season's keywords.

Scroll down to see the latest men's fashion trends from the Spring/Summer 2022 collections.

Hermès

Dust Coats

Sacai

Ermenegildo Zegna

Jumpsuits

Jumpsuits are making a major comeback this season, and we almost missed them. Usually, jumpsuits are influenced by workplace outfits, but this time the jumpsuit transitions from an expansive utilitarian design to a regressive short form.

Giorgio Armani

Lemaire

Easy Suiting

The urban formal suit is always in trend, but this season it takes on a more casual look. This does not diminish its elegance; the slouchy suit is less rigid, more elastic, and comfy without compromising flair. It is a celebration of unstructured suits while without abandoning the overall silhouette.

Alexander McQueen

Louis Vuitton

Vivid Color

Color has made a comeback in recent seasons, and now is the time to incorporate bold colors into your summer ensembles, ranging from pastel to neon. For those who aren't yet ready to stretch their boundaries, a flash of color in an otherwise neutral clothing is a choice.

Fendi

Rick Owens

Cut Outs

Those who wish to be more daring can wear less. Designers this season are introducing a new idea of masculinity through scissor cuts, perforations, indentations, and straps. Cut-outs exemplify an unrestrained and complicated age in men's fashion, as if each building attempt had to be demolished first.

Comme des Garçons

Loewe

Embracing Femininity

Gendered fashion was called into question at the Spring/Summer 2022 men's displays, as traditionally feminine designs were easily blended into fluid menswear. The feminine characteristics are in the details, with an abundance of pinks, skirts, dresses, ruffles, and lace. This season makes you question if these patterns were always solely feminine.

Dsquared2

Ludovic de Saint Sernin

Legs For Days

The legs are the true protagonists this year, capturing all of the attention. This summer, whether lazing on the beach or parading the city streets, legs will be on display in everything from micro-shorts to enormous Bermuda shorts.

Jil Sander

Dolce & Gabbana

Summery Knitwear

The more your sweater resembles anything from your childhood closet, the better. Homemade, youthful, inventive, and bright, novelty knitwear is an early summer essential when matched with lighter apparel pieces.

Lanvin

Dries Van Noten

Prints Galore

With tie-dye, drawings, patchwork, graffiti, geometric patterns, and flowers, this season's prints elevate art to a higher level.

Saint Laurent

Off-White

Black & White

Nothing could be more lovely than the traditional blend of these two opposing hues. This color palette is meant to be simple, but brave users may take black-and-white to new heights with a little ingenuity.

Hermès

Stefan Cooke

Showing Skin

At the height of the summer, clothes might be so translucent that you may not notice the garment beneath. The Spring/Summer 2022 collections exemplify this, with lacy detailing, transparent materials, and flamboyant indiscretion of the ensembles.

Missoni

Amiri

Fashion Leagues

This year, sportswear is the clear victor of the summer season. Tracksuits, jogging suits, hooded sweatshirts, football, basketball...the list of sportswear inspirations is endless. The varsity jacket, the iconic collegiate jacket frequently in colors symbolizing numerous American university teams, is this season's most popular edition.
