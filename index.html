Fashion

Every rising trend on the Spring/Summer 2022 men's runways appears to have been put in place to get you out of the lengthy, never-ending winter. Airy silhouettes, dramatic cut-outs, and a casual aesthetic are just three of the season's keywords.

Creative directors and stylists returned to exhibit their designs for the Spring/Summer 2022 season. The women's fashion collections displayed throughout the fashion weeks have established the new trends for the upcoming hot season, with fresh proposals, excellent returns, and surprising feminine trends. Fashion companies have updated wardrobe basics, while certain labels have maintained their fascination in graphics and floral motifs. Fringes and corsets have also made a comeback, and shapes are becoming more architectural, resembling sculptural forms.

Tips and Tricks

What are weddings, baptisms, networking gatherings, funerals, job interviews, picnics, holiday parties, baby and bridal showers all about? It's always difficult to decide what to wear to them. What you dress to any of these events is determined by a variety of circumstances, including the location, the formality of the invitation, the weather, and, in many cases, what everyone else is planning to wear. However, there are certain broad sartorial norms to which you might refer for help. (Remember, most dress code "laws" are more like guidelines—with a few exceptions, such as not wearing white to a wedding.)

Real Simple fashion editors and experts break down and interpret every potential event dress code, including practical recommendations, etiquette solutions, and outfit choices, to put an end to the what-do-I-wear conundrum once and for all.
